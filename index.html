Why Shamrock?

Purpose-built for communities that live on both Discord and Roblox.

Managing a cross-platform community is hard. Keeping roles in sync, verifying identities, and managing permissions across two platforms usually means manual work or fragile scripts. Shamrock automates it all — so you can focus on growing your community instead of chasing spreadsheets.

🔗

Secure Account Linking

Seamlessly link Discord accounts to Roblox profiles with end-to-end security.

✅

Verification Systems

Automated role assignment based on Roblox group ranks or in-game statistics.

⚙️

Admin Tools

Integrated commands to manage user permissions and access across both platforms.

Performance Focused

Built for stability and speed, ensuring reliable synchronization at scale.
